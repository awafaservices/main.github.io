About Us

Your satisfaction is our reward.

We've proven track record in the delivery of a quality service and has maintained a number of clients for over thirteen years.

Awafa Services is a specialist, privately owned general maintenance and cleaning services company, with a wealth of experience for over thirteen years and is totally dedicated to providing a quality and cost effective maintenance and cleaning service. 

We have been in this business core since 2008 as JSH Group located in Abu Dhabi and over the period of thirteen years we have gained wide range of experience in the different fields of building maintenance (Plumbing-Electrical-CCTV installation, HVAC and Cleaning Services) and facility management.

Furthermore, in our effort to always strive for service excellence we have developed our new firm named as Awafa General Maintenance and Cleaning Services with unique Standard Operating Procedure, which has proven effective for so many years in consistently meeting our clients unique needs We have well-trained and well-motivated employees so that we can provide our clients with the Highest Quality Service That they deserve.
